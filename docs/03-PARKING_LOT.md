# Engineering Excellence Playbook

# 03 - Parking Lot

## Purpose

The Parking Lot captures valuable ideas that are intentionally **not** part of the current roadmap.

This prevents scope creep while ensuring good ideas are not forgotten.

Ideas remain here until they are reviewed during roadmap planning.

---

# Review Policy

The Parking Lot is reviewed:

- At the end of every major version
- Before starting a new roadmap milestone
- When priorities change significantly

Adding an idea to the Parking Lot is **not** a commitment to implement it.

# Admission Criteria

A new idea may enter the roadmap only if it:

- Supports the repository mission.
- Solves a real engineering problem.
- Fits the current roadmap.
- Can be maintained over time.
- Adds long-term value.

Otherwise, it remains in the Parking Lot.

---

# Guiding Principle

Focus is a competitive advantage.

Every feature added means something else will receive less attention.

The Engineering Excellence Playbook grows through deliberate decisions, not by collecting every interesting idea.
